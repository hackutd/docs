HackUTD is the largest hackathon in Texas, organized by students at The University of Texas at Dallas. Each year we bring together thousands of hackers to build, learn, and ship over a single weekend.

This site is the centralized documentation for all of HackUTD's open source projects: the tools and platforms we build to run our events and share with the community. Browse the projects below to get started.
